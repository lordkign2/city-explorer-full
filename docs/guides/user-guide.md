# User Guide

Welcome to City Explorer! This guide will help you make the most of our AI-powered travel platform.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the homepage at [cityexplorer.com](https://cityexplorer.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Fill in your details:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Log in to your new account

### Logging In

1. Visit the homepage
2. Click "Log In" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"

If you've forgotten your password:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Follow the instructions to reset your password

## Exploring Cities

### Browsing Cities

1. From the homepage, browse featured cities
2. Use the search bar to find specific cities
3. Filter cities by:
   - Category (beach, historical, nightlife, etc.)
   - Region
   - Popularity

### City Pages

Each city page provides comprehensive information:
- Overview and description
- Key facts and statistics
- Interactive map
- Top attractions, restaurants, and hotels
- Weather information
- Fun facts and trivia

### Saving Favorite Cities

1. Navigate to a city page
2. Click the heart icon to save to favorites
3. Access your favorites from the "My Favorites" link in your profile menu

## AI-Powered Itineraries

### Generating an Itinerary

1. Navigate to a city page
2. Click "Create Itinerary" or "AI Trip Planner"
3. Specify your preferences:
   - Number of days
   - Budget level (low, medium, high)
   - Interests (culture, food, nature, etc.)
   - Travel dates
4. Click "Generate Itinerary"
5. Review and customize your AI-generated plan

### Customizing Your Itinerary

1. Click "Edit" on any day
2. Add or remove activities
3. Adjust timing
4. Add personal notes
5. Save your changes

### Saving and Sharing Itineraries

1. Click "Save Itinerary" to store it in your profile
2. Use "Share" to generate a link for friends
3. Export as PDF, JSON, or calendar file (premium feature)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Map Not Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Disable ad blockers temporarily

#### Itinerary Not Generating
- Check your internet connection
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Try again in a few minutes
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### Login Problems
- Verify your email and password
- Reset your password if needed
- Check if your account is suspended
- Contact support for assistance
